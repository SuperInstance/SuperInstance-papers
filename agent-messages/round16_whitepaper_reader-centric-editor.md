### Phase 3: Reader-Centric Editing Principles

**Technical Accessibility:**
- Lead with intuition before formal definitions
- Provide concrete examples before abstractions
- Progress from simple to complex gradually
- Insert "concept checks" for self-validation

**Structural Improvements:**
- Add reader roadmaps at section starts
- Include executive summaries for busy readers
- Cross-reference between papers strategically
- Balance section lengths for pacing

**Language Optimization:**
- Replace jargon with plain language when possible
- Introduce technical terms with context
- Use active voice for clarity
- Shorten complex sentence structures

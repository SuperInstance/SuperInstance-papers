### Key Findings
1. **Confidence Model:** Three-zone system (GREEN/YELLOW/RED) provides effective decision boundaries
2. **Tile Composition:** Flexible architecture enables complex workflow construction
3. **Distributed Execution:** Federation strategies achieve Byzantine fault tolerance
4. **Scalability:** Logarithmic overhead in distributed scenarios
5. **Stability:** SMPbot outputs remain stable under model variations

- SuperInstance concept for universal cellular computation
- Confidence cascade for uncertainty propagation
- Stigmergy-based coordination without central authority

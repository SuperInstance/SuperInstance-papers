## ⚠️ Key Risks

### Technical
- **Performance** (HIGH): Progressive enhancement, caching
- **Memory** (HIGH): Hard limits (50 agents max)
- **Compatibility** (MEDIUM): Feature detection

### Business
- **Microsoft** (MEDIUM): Move faster, open source
- **Adoption** (MEDIUM): Clear onboarding, templates
- **Monetization** (LOW): Freemium, enterprise

### Legal
- **Privacy** (HIGH): Local-first, GDPR compliance
- **IP** (LOW): Prior art search, legal review
